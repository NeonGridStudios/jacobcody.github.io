# MindCachèAI — Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: [INSERT DATE]**

MindCachèAI is designed with privacy as a core principle. This app does not track you, identify you, or send your data to any external servers. All recordings and transcripts remain fully on your device unless you explicitly choose to share them.

## Information We Collect
**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

MindCachèAI performs all processing locally on your device. The following information is stored only on your device and never leaves it:

- Audio recordings you create  
- Transcripts generated from your recordings  
- Summaries generated from transcripts  
- Entry titles and notes  

This data is **not** uploaded, shared, analyzed, or stored by the developer.

## Third-Party Access
MindCachèAI does not use third-party analytics, trackers, or external APIs.  
No third party receives or processes your data.

## Optional Sharing
If you choose to use the share feature, the content you share leaves your device under your explicit control. The app does not share anything automatically.

## Security
MindCachèAI supports Face ID / Touch ID to protect access to your entries.  
All data is stored using the standard protections offered by iOS.

## Children’s Privacy
MindCachèAI does not collect any information and is safe for users of all ages.
